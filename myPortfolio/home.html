#about-me

Hi, I'm Aleks, a results-driven front-end developer. I live in Darmstadt, Germany, and specialize in creating visually impressive, responsive websites that provide an excellent user experience. I have developed a number of websites for my portfolio, covering various industries, and this number is steadily growing. Let's collaborate to bring your idea to life.

I am passionate about transforming my creativity and knowledge into successful websites. I’ve helped various clients build their online presence and stay ahead of competitors by using the latest technologies and frameworks.

My journey into web development and IT began long ago. Initially, I learned from free lessons on YouTube, gradually diving deeper into the basics. Soon after, I purchased my first full-fledged course on JavaScript. To further improve my skills, I completed the 'High-Quality Web Interface Development' course by one of the best teachers in Ukraine, Yevhen Andrikanych. After completing my studies, I focused on building my own portfolio, which I continue to work on to this day.
